# Case Study: Automotive Commercial in an LED Studio — What Virtual Production Delivers

The automotive sector is one of the hardest to execute in traditional post-production. Reflections on bodywork, headlight glare, undercarriage shadows — each of these elements demands hours of compositing work, and the result still often looks artificial. An LED studio solves this at the source.

## The Problem LED Solves

In the traditional approach, a car is filmed against green screen or on location. The environment (mountain road, city at dusk, motorway) is then added in post-production. The result: reflections on the bodywork don't match the virtual environment, shadows fall in the wrong direction, and the paint looks like it is floating in space rather than living within it.

In an LED studio, the environment is displayed directly on the screen behind the car. The camera sees what the eye sees: a car in a real environment, with real light reflected from the LED wall, with real reflections on the windscreen and bodywork.

## What You Gain on Set

**Natural reflections and highlights** — the LED wall emits the actual lighting environment. Metallic paint reflects the landscape colours exactly as it would on location. This cannot be faked without LED.

**No masking or keying** — no cutting the car out of a green screen, fighting green spill on chrome elements, or correcting edge fringing on the bodywork.

**The director sees the final image** — on the preview monitor you see exactly what will appear on screen. Decisions are made on set, not three weeks later in post-production.

**The actor/driver reacts to the environment** — if they're driving through a tunnel in the background, the car interior actually darkens. Expressions and reactions are natural because the environment is real.

## Comparison: LED Studio vs Traditional Production

| Element | LED Studio | Green Screen + VFX |
|---------|------------|-------------------|
| Bodywork reflections | ✅ Natural | ❌ Rendered, often incorrect |
| Post-production time | ✅ Minimal | ❌ 3–6 weeks |
| Environment changes | ✅ Instant on set | ❌ Costly iteration |
| Number of shoot days | ✅ 1–2 days | ⚠️ 1–2 days + locations |
| Total cost | ✅ Comparable or lower | ❌ Higher after VFX costs |

## Who Is This For?

LED virtual production works for: car brands (launches, seasonal campaigns, dealer content), advertising agencies producing TV and digital spots, production houses delivering premium content for automotive, fashion and tech brands.

If your production requires perfect reflections, dynamic environments or rapid on-set iterations — this is the tool for you.
