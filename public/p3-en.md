# Outdoor LED Screen with Black-SMD Technology: Why 8,500:1 Contrast Changes Everything

## The Problem with Outdoor Screens in Direct Sunlight

You know the view: a festival, mid-summer, direct sun blasting the screen, and the image looks bleak. Colors are washed out, and true blacks are just a pale gray.
That's physics—and the limits of older lighting modules which use bright housing to emit their light.

Our outdoor screen operates using **Black SMD1515**.

## What is Black SMD1515?

The coating, base, and entire pixel structure are deeply black.
1. It absorbs, rather than reflects, outside light.
2. A black pixel isn't just turned off—it has no bright housing that could catch a reflection.
3. A massive leap in image contrast.

Thanks to this, the **Absen NT3.9W V2** boasts an **8,500:1 contrast ratio** — an absolutely top-tier metric in the outdoor LED market.
