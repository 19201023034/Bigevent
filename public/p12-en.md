# LED Screen for Corporate Events and Conferences — A Modern Stage for Your Brand

A corporate event is not just about content — it is a statement about your company's values and professionalism. The screen behind the speaker, the gala backdrop, the financial results visualisation — all of this is part of your brand image. And here an LED screen makes a difference a projector simply cannot match.

## Formats Where LED Is Irreplaceable

**Annual Conference / AGM** — a stage with a 6–10 m wide LED screen as a backdrop behind the podium. Financial data, video, presentations — everything on one seamless surface without a frame and without projection.

**Product Launch** — the premiere of a new product is the moment of maximum audience attention. An LED wall as the main stage gives you full control over mood, colour and the drama of the reveal moment.

**Town Hall / All-Hands Meeting** — for companies with large teams, an LED screen ensures that every participant — whether seated in the front row or by the wall — sees a full-quality image.

**Gala / Dinner / Award Ceremony** — during dinners and award ceremonies, an LED screen forms a backdrop that builds atmosphere. Event photography looks professional regardless of room lighting.

## Why LED and Not a Projector for a Conference?

In a typical conference room, lighting is always a compromise: bright enough for participants, but damaging for projection. An LED screen has no such problem. You can have a fully lit room and a perfect image on screen simultaneously.

Added bonus: no HDMI cable across the floor, no projector hanging over guests' heads, no cooling fans in the background of your audio recordings.

The screen accepts any signal source: laptop via HDMI, conferencing system, video playback, live camera feed. You can display PowerPoint and Keynote presentations, 4K video content, live camera broadcast, branding graphics and logo animations.

You don't need special software — just what you use every day.
