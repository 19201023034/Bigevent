# How Much Does LED Screen Rental Cost? A Complete Pricing Guide

LED screen rental is one of the first things clients ask about — and one of the hardest to quote without context. The price of an LED wall depends on several key factors. In this article we break it down component by component.

## What Makes Up the Cost of Renting an LED Screen?

The total rental cost is the sum of several positions you should understand before sending an enquiry:

**1. Screen Surface Area (m²)**
This is the biggest cost driver. The larger the wall, the more panels, power distribution and rigging are required. A 4×2.5 m screen is 10 m² — a 8×4.5 m screen is 36 m², meaning over 3× more panels.

**2. Technology (Pixel Pitch)**
A P1.9 panel (indoor, fine-pitch) costs more than P3.9 (outdoor). If your event is in a conference room with guests sitting closer than 4 metres from the screen — P1.9 is a necessity. For outdoor events or large stages, P3.9 is the optimal choice.

**3. Duration (Number of Days)**
Rental is counted from delivery to collection. One event day usually means 3 logistical days: delivery and installation, event day, dismantle and return transport.

**4. Transport and Logistics**
Distance from our warehouse (Aachen/NRW) to the event venue. Local events across the NRW region are more cost-effective than assignments requiring cross-border transport.

**5. On-Site Technical Support**
A professional LED technician present throughout the event is a standard — not an option. It guarantees system reliability and instant response to any technical issue.

**6. Signal Processor and Controller**
Every screen requires an image processor (e.g. NovaStar MX40 Pro). For straightforward setups it is included in the base price. Complex multi-source signal configurations may require additional setup time.
