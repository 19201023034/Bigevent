# Hannover Messe, bauma Munich, Interpack — Local LED Screen Rental from NRW

Germany is the largest trade fair market in Europe. Hannover Messe, bauma, Interpack, Ambiente, drupa — these events attract tens of thousands of exhibitors from around the world. Renting an LED screen for your booth is standard practice today. But where do you source it without spending half your budget on transport?

## Why Your Supplier's Location Matters

LED screens are bulky equipment. A standard booth with a 4×2.5 m screen requires 1–2 pallets of cargo, 2–3 technical staff and a truck with a tail lift. Shipping from far away means:
- 800–2000 EUR one-way transport cost (route + driver + fuel)
- Risk of border delays or traffic holdups
- Crew subsistence and accommodation (40–80 EUR/person/night is the lower end during trade fairs)
- Limited flexibility — you arrive once and can't quickly return in case of a technical issue

## Documents and Certificates Required by German Venues

German trade fair venues require AV suppliers to provide:
- **B1 fire resistance certificate** for LED panels and cabling
- **Structural calculations** for suspended (flown) screens
- **Certified hoists and rigging** (SWL confirmed)
- **Public liability insurance** at the required coverage level

All our equipment meets these requirements. Documentation is prepared on request from the organiser.
